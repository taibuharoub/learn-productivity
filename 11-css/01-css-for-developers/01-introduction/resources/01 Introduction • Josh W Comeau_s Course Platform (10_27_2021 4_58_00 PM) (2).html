Getting the most out of this course

This course is structured a little bit differently from most other courses.

Instead of focusing on how to build specific layouts, we really try and focus on the generalized principles. I don't just want to show you how to build a handful of common layouts, I want to teach you the tools needed to build any layout.

To accomplish this goal, you'll occasionally be challenged to build things you haven't explicitly been shown how to build. A lot of the exercises in this course are intended to get you thinking, to help you form connections in your brain.

If possible, I highly recommend cultivating a “growth mindset”. There may be times in this course that you struggle to complete an exercise or workshop, and having a growth mindset can transform a frustrating experience into a productive one.

But, I also want to meet people where they are. You can use this course however works best for you. Here are some alternative ways you can use this course:

  • Watch the solution videos first, and then go back and see if you can tackle the problem
  • Code alongside the videos with a split-screen, tackling it line-by-line with me.

The most important thing is that you have fun with this course. This is a comprehensive course, with a ton of content: if you're not having fun, you probably won't complete it.

Learning in public

Here's something I forgot to mention in the video: learning in public is encouraged!

Please feel free to stream your progress on Twitch or YouTube, or share images/GIFs from the course on Twitter or anywhere else.

You can use the hashtag #CSSforJS if you'd like! 🐦

Learning in public is a great way to solidify what you know. It's also great for accountability, to give you motivation to keep learning!

Housekeeping

Receipts

You should have received a copy of your receipt by email. It will have been sent from help@paddle.com.

If you don't see a receipt in your inbox, you can access it on the “Account” page, or you can reach out to support for a copy.

The receipt is web-based, so that you can add any information you need (name, address, VAT / sales tax info). You can download a PDF through the “Print” interface (File -> Print -> Save as PDF).

Device support

This course is designed to be completed on a desktop or laptop computer.

This isn't a passive video course; there are tons of interactive elements and challenges. For best results, you'll want to attempt these challenges from a device with a large screen. Some things might not work properly on mobile devices.

That said, I am aware that it's becoming more and more common to code from mobile/tablet devices, and am working towards being fully responsive.
